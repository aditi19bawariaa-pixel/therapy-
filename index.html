A little about me

Rooted in truth.
Not scripts.

I got into this work because I genuinely believe that most people are not broken. They are misunderstood, sometimes by the world around them and often by themselves. That quiet feeling of not quite fitting, not quite being seen or not quite knowing why you feel the way you do is something I take seriously.

I am Aditi, a Psychologist based in Bhopal and Jabalpur. I work with individuals, couples and young professionals who are at a point where something inside them is asking for more clarity, more honesty or simply more peace.

What I can promise you is honesty. Not the comfortable kind, but the kind that actually helps. And I will be right there with you through all of it.

How I work

What makes this
different

Three principles that guide every session whether we are meeting for the first time or the fiftieth.

01

You before the technique

I work with CBT, ACT and Art Therapy among other approaches but I will never push a technique onto you before I understand you as a person. The conversation is not a warm up, it is the work. Everything else follows only when it is genuinely needed.

02

No forced positivity

I do not ask you to reframe your pain or look for silver linings. I sit with the bitterness that life sometimes is because I genuinely believe that being met honestly in a difficult moment does more for a person than being rushed toward feeling better.

03

Depth over speed

Real understanding takes time. I do not rush toward solutions. I stay with you in the difficult parts because that is usually where the most important things are.

Questions

Things people
usually ask

Still unsure? Reach out directly. There is no pressure and no wrong question.

WhatsApp me directly
I have never done therapy before. Is that okay?+
Absolutely. Most of my clients come in with no prior therapy experience. There is no prerequisite, just a willingness to be honest. The first session is always a conversation and not a commitment.
Are sessions confidential?+
Yes. Everything shared in a session stays between us. Confidentiality is the foundation of this work. The only exceptions are situations where safety is at risk and those would always be discussed with you first.
Is this a queer friendly space?+
Yes, completely. I work with individuals across all gender identities and sexual orientations. Who you are is never something you need to explain or justify here. This is a space for everyone.
Will I feel better after just one session?+
Therapy is not magic and I will never promise you it is. It is deep, rooted work that takes time and commitment. What one session can give you is clarity, a sense of being heard and a place to begin. The rest we build together.
Do you work with men as well?+
Yes, I work with all genders. The issues I support including anxiety, relationships, career and identity are universal. You are welcome regardless of gender, age or background.
What platform do online sessions happen on?+
Online sessions are conducted over Google Meet or Zoom, whichever you are more comfortable with. All you need is a quiet space and a stable internet connection.
How many sessions will I need?+
There is no fixed number. Some people find significant clarity in three to four sessions while others prefer ongoing support. We will figure out what makes sense for you together without any pressure to commit upfront.
Is there a sliding scale for students?+
Yes. I understand that finances can be a barrier especially for students. Reduced rates are available so just mention it when you reach out and we will work something out.
